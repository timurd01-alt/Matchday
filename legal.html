Privacy policy

Matchday does not require an account, payment information, or a real name to use its current local-first version. We do not run advertising trackers or sell personal information.

Information stored on your device

The app uses your browser’s local and session storage to remember language, selected sport, appearance settings, favourite team, watchlist, dismissed welcome screen, and your personal “Beat the Model” picks and results. A randomly generated device identifier and an optional display handle may also be stored locally for the community feature. This information remains in your browser unless you enable a future online leaderboard or clear site data.

Information sent to other services

The app loads sports data prepared by Matchday and may load Google Fonts. When you open a news story or an external data-provider link, that third party receives information under its own privacy policy. Hosting providers may also process ordinary technical logs, such as IP address, browser type, and request time, to operate and secure a hosted site.

Online leaderboard

It is disabled in the current release. If it is enabled later, Matchday would send the pseudonymous device identifier, optional handle, and aggregated pick statistics needed to operate the leaderboard. Before enabling it publicly, the publisher will update this page with the host, retention period, and contact details for that service.

Your choices

You can clear locally stored Matchday information through your browser’s site-data controls. This policy will be updated before any accounts, payments, marketing analytics, or public community profiles are introduced.
